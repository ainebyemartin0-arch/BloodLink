About BloodLink

Connecting blood donors with patients in need at St. Francis Hospital Nsambya, Kampala

Our Mission

BloodLink is a life-saving platform that connects voluntary blood donors with patients who need urgent blood transfusions at St. Francis Hospital Nsambya. We bridge the critical gap between blood supply and demand through innovative technology and community engagement.

How BloodLink Works

1. Register

Sign up as a blood donor with your details and blood type. Registration takes just 2 minutes.

2. Receive SMS Alert

When your blood type is urgently needed, you'll receive an instant SMS alert from the hospital.

3. Save a Life

Visit St. Francis Hospital Nsambya and donate blood to help save a patient's life.

Our Impact

  • 24/7 Availability: Round-the-clock blood donation coordination
  • Quick Response: Average response time under 30 minutes
  • All Blood Types: We support all blood types (A+, A-, B+, B-, O+, O-, AB+, AB-)
  • Verified Donors: All donors are registered and verified

About St. Francis Hospital Nsambya

St. Francis Hospital Nsambya is a leading healthcare facility in Kampala, Uganda, providing quality medical care for over 100 years. Our partnership ensures:

  • Professional medical supervision during donations
  • Safe and sterile donation procedures
  • Post-donation care and refreshments
  • Regular health check-ups for donors

Donation Requirements

Eligibility:
  • Age: 18-65 years
  • Weight: Minimum 50kg
  • Good health condition
  • No recent illnesses or infections
Before Donation:
  • Get adequate sleep (8 hours)
  • Eat a healthy meal
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Avoid alcohol 24 hours before
