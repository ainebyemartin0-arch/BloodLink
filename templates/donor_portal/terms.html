2. Eligibility Requirements

To register and use BloodLink as a blood donor, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Be in good general health
  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Not have any disqualifying medical conditions
  • Meet minimum weight requirements (50kg/110lbs)
  • Have valid identification

BloodLink reserves the right to verify eligibility and decline registration for any reason.

3. Donor Responsibilities

As a registered donor, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information about your health and medical history
  • Update your information promptly if any changes occur
  • Respond to emergency blood requests when your blood type is needed
  • Follow all donation eligibility guidelines provided by healthcare professionals
  • Maintain confidentiality of your donation activities
  • Report any adverse reactions from blood donation

4. Donation Process

Blood donation is a voluntary process that involves:

  • Health screening and verification before each donation
  • Collection of approximately 450ml of whole blood
  • Post-donation monitoring and care
  • Testing of donated blood for safety and compatibility
  • Storage and distribution according to medical standards

You may decline to donate at any time without penalty.

5. Emergency Response System

When you receive an emergency blood request notification:

  • You will receive SMS notifications when your blood type is urgently needed
  • Response time is critical - please respond promptly
  • Your contact information may be shared with the requesting healthcare facility
  • You can accept or decline requests based on your availability and health status
  • All communications will be logged for quality assurance

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
